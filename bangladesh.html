Perceptions of climate hazards and risks

Communities are very concerned about climate change, particularly about heat waves, river erosion, cyclones, floods, and salinity intrusion as these impacts directly affect livelihoods, health and whatever little assets the communities have. But different people face different impacts from climate change, so we need tailored interventions.
